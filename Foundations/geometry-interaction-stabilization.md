# Geometry → Interaction → Stabilization

*Linking AI probability mechanics to interaction-level failure modes*

---

## Purpose

To connect:

- how AI systems generate outputs (geometry, probability)  
with  
- how those outputs are used and stabilized in interaction  

---

## Step 1: Geometry (System Level)

AI models do not store or retrieve facts.

They:

- represent meaning as vectors in high-dimensional space  
- navigate probability distributions  
- generate outputs by following likely trajectories  

> Output = probabilistic coherence, not grounded truth

---

## Step 2: Interaction (Human–AI Loop)

When a user engages with output:

- framing is introduced  
- responses are interpreted  
- ideas are reinforced or questioned  

Interaction determines whether the output is:

- tested  
- refined  
- or accepted  

---

## Step 3: Stabilization

If interaction:

- reinforces coherence  
- reduces questioning  
- repeats the same framing  

Then:

> probabilistic outputs become stabilized narratives

---

## Key Mechanism

> AI produces coherence  
> Interaction determines whether that coherence is corrected or stabilized  

---

## Failure Condition

> Stabilized incoherence

Occurs when:

- output is coherent  
- interaction reinforces it  
- correction is not introduced  

---

## Important Distinction

- The model does not “decide to be wrong”  
- The user does not “intend to be misled”  

Failure emerges from:

> system output × interaction dynamics  

---

## Correction Path

To prevent stabilization:

- introduce oscillation  
- apply reversal  
- maintain diagnostic questioning  

---

## Practical Implication

Understanding model geometry is necessary but not sufficient.

> Without interaction-level correction, probabilistic outputs can become stable but incorrect beliefs

---

## Summary

AI systems generate probabilistic trajectories through a geometric space.

Interaction determines whether those trajectories:

- remain open to correction  
- or become stabilized as truth  

---

## Final Principle

> AI produces probabilistic coherence  
> Interaction determines whether it becomes stabilized incoherence
