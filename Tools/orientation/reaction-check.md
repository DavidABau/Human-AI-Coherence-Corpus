# Reaction Check

*A quick tool for recognizing reactive thinking.*

When emotions are intense, the mind often runs automatic reactions.

Use the following questions to check your thinking.

---

## 1. Certainty Check

Am I assuming I am completely right?

Extreme certainty can sometimes signal defensive thinking.

---

## 2. Blame Check

Am I placing all responsibility on someone else?

Complex situations rarely have a single cause.

---

## 3. Avoidance Check

Am I trying to avoid thinking about something uncomfortable?

Avoidance can temporarily reduce stress but often increases confusion later.

---

## 4. Control Check

Am I trying to control something outside my control?

Recognizing limits often reduces frustration.

---

## 5. Reflection Check

Ask yourself:

> What reaction am I having right now?

Simply noticing the reaction can reduce its intensity.

---

## Reminder

You are not your reactions.  
You are the one who can notice them.
