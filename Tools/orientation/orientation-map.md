# Orientation Map

*A simple model for understanding how people move from confusion back to clarity.*

---

## The Orientation Loop

Life often unfolds in a repeating pattern:


Life Situation
↓
Human Pressures
(Limitation • Vulnerability • Change • Loss)
↓
Mental Reaction
(defense, avoidance, certainty, blame)
↓
Reflection
(noticing the reaction)
↓
Clearer Response
↓
Greater Stability (Coherence)


---

## How to Use This Map

When feeling overwhelmed or confused, ask:

### 1. What pressure am I encountering?

Examples:

- uncertainty
- criticism
- unexpected change
- relationship tension

### 2. What reaction am I having?

Examples:

- anger
- fear
- avoidance
- blaming others

### 3. Can I pause and observe the reaction?

Simply noticing the reaction often reduces its intensity.

---

## Key Insight

Reflection creates space between **reaction** and **response**.

That space allows clarity to return.
