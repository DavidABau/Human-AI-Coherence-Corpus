# Rapid Reorientation

*A simple 2-minute reset for moments of anxiety, panic, or confusion.*

---

## Step 1 — Pause

Stop for a moment.

Take three slow breaths.

There is no need to solve the situation yet.

---

## Step 2 — Notice the Situation

Ask yourself:

> What is happening right now?

Describe the situation simply and factually.

---

## Step 3 — Notice Your Reaction

Ask:

> What reaction am I having?

Examples:

- anger
- anxiety
- defensiveness
- fear

Simply noticing the reaction often reduces its intensity.

---

## Step 4 — Check Assumptions

Ask:

> Am I assuming something that might not be fully true?

Reactions often intensify because of unexamined assumptions.

---

## Step 5 — Re-orient

Remind yourself:

- uncertainty is part of life
- strong reactions are normal
- reactions do not have to control your response

Then ask:

> What is the most reasonable next step?

Focus only on the **next step**, not the entire problem.

---

## Reminder

You are not your reactions.  
You are the one who can notice them.
