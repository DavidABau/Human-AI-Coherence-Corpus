# Fragmentation Warning Signs

*A quick tool for recognizing when thinking may be becoming distorted.*

---

## What Fragmentation Means

Fragmentation occurs when thoughts, emotions, and reactions stop aligning clearly with reality.

This often happens during stress or conflict.

Recognizing it early helps restore clarity.

---

## Warning Signs

### Extreme Certainty

Believing you are completely right and dismissing other perspectives.

---

### Blame Fixation

Believing one person or group is entirely responsible for the problem.

---

### Emotional Overload

Strong emotions make it difficult to think clearly.

Examples:

- rage
- panic
- defensiveness

---

### Avoidance

Feeling the urge to avoid thinking about a difficult issue.

---

### Identity Defense

Feeling personally threatened by disagreement.

Example:

> "If this belief is wrong, then I must be wrong."

---

## What To Do

Pause and ask:

> What reaction am I having right now?

Reflection often restores clarity.
