# Relationship Compass

People often narrow toward different stabilisation strategies under stress.

This shapes how they respond to others.

## Common Orientations

Connection  
Competence  
Control  
Understanding  
Meaning  
Security  
Autonomy  

## Core Insight

People tend to offer what *stabilises them*.

Example:

Someone narrowed toward **Competence / Control** is unlikely to validate.

They may:
- solve
- explain
- redirect
- talk about their own experience

This is often misread as lack of care.

But it may simply be their stabilisation style.

## What this tool helps explain

- Why some people fix instead of comfort
- Why some people withdraw
- Why some people talk about themselves during loss
- Why some people seek reassurance vs independence

It describes orientation — not personality.
