# HACC Interaction Pocket Card

## 1. What is needed right now?

Regulation → Help me settle  
Bonding → Be with me  
Validation → See me  
Narrative → Let me express  
Diagnostic → Help me understand / fix  

---

## 2. If tension appears, ask:

Am I responding to a different need than the one being expressed?

---

## 3. What stabilises this person?

Connection  
Competence  
Control  
Understanding  
Meaning  
Security  
Autonomy  

People tend to offer what stabilises them.

Example:  
Competence / Control → likely to fix, not validate.

---

## 4. Simple Adjustment Order

Regulation before Meaning  
Bonding before Advice  
Validation before Diagnostic  

---

Most conflict is mismatch, not bad intent.
