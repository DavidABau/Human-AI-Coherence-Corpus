# HACC Practical Tools

These tools are designed for **real-time human interaction**, not theory.

They are self-contained and usable independently. 

### “No prior corpus knowledge required”

They help answer simple questions like:

- What’s happening right now?
- What is needed?
- Why is this interaction failing or flowing?

Start with whichever feels most useful:

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|--------|
| 5 Modes | Understand conversational needs |
| 12 Modes | Understand conversational nuance |
| Relationship Compass | Understand relational orientation |
| Quick Reference | One-page real-time guide |

⚠️ These are not personality systems or identity labels.  
They are **situational navigation tools**.

Use lightly.

Maps are rafts — not reality.
