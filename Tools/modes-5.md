# The 5 Modes

A simple model for understanding **what kind of response is needed** in a conversation.

Miscommunication often happens when the response matches the wrong need.

## The Modes

**Regulation** → "Help me settle"  
**Bonding** → "Be with me"  
**Validation** → "See me"  
**Narrative** → "Let me express"  
**Diagnostic** → "Help me understand or fix"

## Core Insight

Most conflict is **mode mismatch**, not bad intent.

Example:

Someone expressing Narrative may receive Diagnostic.

They wanted expression.  
They got analysis.

This feels like being unheard — even if help was intended.
