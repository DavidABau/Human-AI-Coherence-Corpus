# Alignment Expression Channels
## Interaction Scaffolding Module

### Purpose

People frequently misinterpret *how* values are expressed as *whether* values exist.

This module provides a lightweight interpretive tool for distinguishing:

Alignment absence  
vs  
Alignment expressed through a different behavioural channel.

It supports:

- Regulation
- Bonding
- Validation
- Reduced moral misread during interaction

---

## Core Principle

Alignment is enacted behaviourally.

But enactment is filtered through cognitive style.

Different individuals express care, responsibility, integrity, or growth through different behavioural channels.

Misreading the channel often leads to:

- Premature moralisation
- Relational breakdown
- Narrative inflation (“they don’t care”)

---

## The Channels

These are expression styles, not identity types and not moral rankings.

| Channel | Behavioural Expression | Protects | Common Misread | Shadow / Distortion |
|---------|-----------------------|----------|----------------|---------------------|
| Duty | Reliability, consistency | Stability | Rigid | Punitive rule-enforcement |
| Care | Emotional attunement | Wellbeing | Soft | Boundary loss / enabling |
| Practical | Problem solving | Function | Detached | Instrumental dismissal |
| Strategic | Long-term optimisation | Future outcomes | Cold | Ends-justify-means logic |
| Experiential | Presence & immediacy | Human warmth | Superficial | Impulsivity / avoidance |
| Authentic | Personal integrity | Inner truth | Self-centred | Intransigence / anti-cooperation |
| Evolutionary | Disruption of stagnation | Growth | Reckless | Destabilisation-for-its-own-sake |
| Interpersonal | Group shaping | Cohesion | Controlling | Approval-driven manipulation |

---

## Shadow Dynamics

Shadow does not imply immorality.

It reflects:

Channel under stress becoming overprotective.

| Channel | Healthy | Shadow |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Duty | Stability | Punishment |
| Care | Support | Smothering |
| Practical | Help | Dismissal |
| Strategic | Vision | Dehumanisation |
| Authentic | Integrity | Isolation rigidity |
| Evolutionary | Growth | Chaos-for-ego |
| Interpersonal | Harmony | Control |

---

## Interaction Use

When tension appears, ask:

Is alignment absent, or is the channel mismatched?

Examples:

| Behaviour | Likely Channel | Misread As |
|-----------|---------------|------------|
| Fixes problem without emotional discussion | Practical | Doesn’t care |
| Upholds rule despite discomfort | Duty | Heartless |
| Refuses group compromise | Authentic | Difficult |
| Challenges consensus | Evolutionary | Disruptive |

---

## Safeguard

This module:

- Does not measure morality
- Does not classify people as good or bad
- Does not predict virtue

It only describes:

How alignment tends to be behaviourally expressed.

---

## One-Line Summary

Alignment Expression Channels describe how care shows up, not whether it exists.
